Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

PLAN is a single-list to-do app for iOS. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing. PLAN has no user accounts, no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. There is no server to send data to.

Where your data lives

All list items, themes, and preferences are stored locally on your device, using Apple's SwiftData and UserDefaults frameworks. None of this data leaves your device unless you choose to enable iCloud sync (see below).

iCloud

PLAN's data schema is CloudKit-compatible, and iCloud sync is off by default. If a future version enables iCloud sync and you opt in, your data will sync between your own Apple devices via your personal iCloud account. We never see it — only you and Apple have access, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

AI features

PLAN includes an opt-in "Auto AI summarize" feature that can shorten long item titles on commit. In the current version, this runs against a local stub and sends nothing anywhere. If a future version routes summaries through a remote model, this policy will be updated and you will be notified before any data leaves your device.

Children

PLAN is rated for ages 4+. It does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Because we collect nothing, there is no data to access, export, or delete on our side. You can delete all PLAN data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy: ledfordlabs@gmail.com

Changes

This policy may be updated alongside future app releases. Material changes will be summarized in the App Store "What's New" notes for the affected version.
